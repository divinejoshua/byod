BYOD - Bring Your Own Data

Information re-defined

BYOD (Bring Your Own Data) a powerful tool that uses artificial intelligence to help you fine-tune data from your word documents. With Boyd, you can easily extract and analyze data from your documents, such as names, dates, and numbers, and then use our intuitive interface to make adjustments and corrections as needed.

Our AI algorithms are designed to learn from your input, so the more you use the app, the smarter it becomes. Whether you're a business professional, researcher, or student, Boyd is the perfect tool for anyone who needs to work with large amounts of data in their word documents.
